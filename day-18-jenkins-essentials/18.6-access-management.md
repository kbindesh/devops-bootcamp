# Jenkins Access Control - Authentication and Authorization

- Jenkins access control is divided into two parts:
  1. **Authentication** - users prove who they are. The security realm determines user identity and group memberships.

  2. **Authorization** - answers _what are you authorized to perform_ using authorization strategy? This controls whether a user (directly or through group memberships) has a permission.
