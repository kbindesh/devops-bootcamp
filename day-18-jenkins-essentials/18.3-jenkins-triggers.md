# Understanding `Jenkins Build Triggers`

## What are Build Triggers?

- **Triggers** are the mechanisms to automatically initiate/run the jenkins jobs.
- Following are the types of Jenkins triggers:
  - **Poll SCM (Jenkins Polling Cron Job)**
  - **Scheduled Jobs**
  - **Build Trigger Remote**
  - **Poll SCM (GitHub Webhook)**
  - **Build After Projects Are Built** (Job chaining)

## 01. Poll SCM (Jenkins Polling Cron Job)

- **Poll SCM** provides a mechanism in Jenkins that periodically checks the changes that are made in the SCM (e.g. GitHub).
- If any changes are detected, Jenkins triggers the build and also facilitates CI and automated testing.

### Configure Poll SCM in Jenkins

1. Login to Jenkins Dashboard

2. Create a new Jenkins Job (Freestyle)

3. Select **build trigger** form the left-side panel and check the the **Poll SCM** option

4. Enter the cron expression

- Each expression consists of 5 fields seperated by a tab key or whitespace.

```
# Syntax
MINUTE HOUR DOM MONTH DOW

MINUTE  (0-59)
HOUR (0-23)
DOM (1-31)
MONTH (1-12)
DOW (0-7 | where 0 and 7 are Sunday)
```

- **Examples**

```
# To check/poll the SCM for any new changes every 15 minutes
H/15 * * * *
OR
*/15 * * * *

# To check/poll the SCM for any new changes every 30 minutes
H/30 * * * *

# To check/poll the SCM every 5 min past every hour (5th minute of every Hour)
5 * * * *

# To check/poll the SCM every day at 0800 hours (8AM)
0 8 * * *

# To check/poll the SCM at 4, 6, and 8 o'clock PM every day
0 16,18,20 * * *

# To check/poll the SCM at 5PM and 1AM every day
0 1,17 * * *

# To check/poll the SCM morning at 9AM, during weekdays only
00 09 * * 1-5

# To check/poll the SCM at lunch time (assuming 1PM), during weekdays only
00 13 * * 1-5
```

## 02. Scheduled Jobs

- If you want Jenkins to start a build at a specific time, set up a scheduled job with a cron expression.

```
# Syntax
MINUTE HOUR DOM MONTH DOW

MINUTE  (0-59)
HOUR (0-23)
DOM (1-31)
MONTH (1-12)
DOW (0-7 | where 0 and 7 are Sunday)
```

- **Examples**

```
[Here, "H" means any at any time during the hour]

# Run the Jenkins job every fifteen minutes
H/15 * * * *

# Once every two hours at 45 minutes past the hour starting at 9:45 AM and finishing at 3:45 PM every weekday
45 9-16/2 * * 1-5

# Once in every two hour slot between 8 AM and 4 PM every weekday (i.e 9:38 AM, 11:38 AM, 1:38 PM, 3:38 PM)
H H(8-15)/2 * * 1-5

# Once a day on the 1st and 15th of every month except December
H H 1,15 1-11 *
```
